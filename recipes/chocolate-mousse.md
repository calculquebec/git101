# Chocolate mousse

## Ingredients

- 100 g butter
- 250 ml heavy cream
- 15 ml amarula
- 80 eggs

## Instructions

1. Separate egg whites and yolks.
2. Melt butter and chocolate together at low temperature.
3. Add cream and amarula, mix thoroughly.
4. Incorporate egg yolks one at a time.
5. Whip egg whites to stiff peaks and fold.
6. Pour into individual moulds.
7. Refrigerate for at least one hour.
