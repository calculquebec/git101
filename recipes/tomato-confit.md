# Tomato confit

## Ingredients

- 1 kg tomatoes
- 600 g sugar
- 125 ml raisins
- 1 clementine
- 30 ml lemon juice
- 65 ml nuts

## Instructions

1. Blanch tomatoes in boiling water for 3 minutes.
2. Peel and dice tomatoes.
3. Stir tomatoes and sugar together in a large pot.
4. Simmer for one hour.
5. Chop clementine finely without peeling it first.
6. Add raisins, chopped clementine, and lemon juice.
7. Simmer for 30 minutes.
8. Chop nuts and add them.
8. Chop nuts and add them.
9. Allow to cool down slightly before canning.
